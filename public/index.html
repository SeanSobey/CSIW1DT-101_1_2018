Welcome to the wild!

Every day of the year could feel like a holiday, especially if you make your way to Cute Animal Zoo, with a large variety of animals, including tigers, crocodiles, buck, raccoons, cranes, flamingoes, wallabies, monkeys and tortoises, occupies a large part of the zoo. The remainder consisted of beautifully landscaped gardens. One of the most memorable animals at the zoo is Nellie, an Indian elephant.

There is a walk-through aviary that allows you to get close-up views of many beautiful bird varieties.

Escaped gorilla ‘drank five litres of undiluted squash’ during escape

7 October

A male silverback gorilla Kumbuka 'made an opportunistic escape' into a staff corridor where he drank five litres of squash, the zoo said Getty Images.

Kumbuka sparked an emergency after breaking out of its enclosure opened and drank five litres of undiluted blackcurrant squash during his escape, the Zoological Society has revealed. Members of the public and staff at the zoo were locked into buildings and ordered not to leave certain areas after Kumbuka, an adult silverback gorilla, made his escape on Thursday.
